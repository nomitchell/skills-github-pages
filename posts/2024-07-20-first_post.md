I'm broadly interested in generalizable AI, math and problem solving skills, game playing, and more; these are the topics I think most of my posts will be about, but others might sneak in as my interests change. I hope that the content I post will be interesting for both you and me, and we can both learn as I progress. I'm not certain what my first actual post will be, but I expect it to be up on the 18th of August 2024 (written in my calendar).
